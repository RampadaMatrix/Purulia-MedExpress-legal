Privacy Policy for Purulia MedExpress

Effective Date: 02 January 2026
Last Updated: 02 January 2026

1. Introduction

Purulia MedExpress respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, phone, email)
  • Delivery address and location (with consent)
  • Prescription images and order history
  • Payment transaction identifiers
  • Device and usage analytics

3. How We Use Information

  • Order processing and delivery
  • Prescription sharing with pharmacies
  • Customer support and communication
  • Security and fraud prevention

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with pharmacy partners, delivery partners, service providers, or authorities when legally required.

5. Data Retention

  • Prescriptions: up to 5 years
  • Transaction data: up to 7 years
  • Location data: up to 30 days

6. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, access controls, and monitoring to protect user data.

7. User Rights

  • Access and correction of data
  • Deletion requests
  • Consent management

8. Children’s Privacy

Services are not intended for users under 18 without parental supervision.
