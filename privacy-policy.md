## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Periods

**Medical Records**
- Prescriptions: 5 years (as per law)

**Personal Data**
- Account data: Until deletion request  
- Forum posts: Until deletion or 2 years after last activity  
- Location data: 30 days  

**Payment Data**
- Transaction records: 7 years  
- Billing information: 1 year  

### 6.2 Data Deletion

- Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
